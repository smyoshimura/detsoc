ESCAPE ROOMS

The Grand Detectives Society Escape Rooms are real-life interactive games and fully immersive experiences. You and your chosen team will be locked inside a themed room with 60 minutes to escape and solve the mystery unique to that scenario. Find clues, solve puzzles, uncover dark, soul-defiling secrets, but above all else, ensure your survival. You are the detective, and the greatest mysteries are yours to solve.

Accept Your Training

Under the guidance of our curator, you will learn the history of our Society, the skills necessary to survive, and examine the clues that have brought us together.

Escape the Room

You will be locked inside, surrounded by puzzles, with only your wits and a few choice friends to assist you. One hour to find the key and escape. Will it be enough?

Solve the Mystery

Every puzzle, every clue, points to one final truth. Judge the evidence wisely, lest you face judgment yourself!

BOOKING YOUR ADVENTURE

Bookings may be scheduled online through the Detectives Society website. Choose the room you wish to book, select an available time slot, and specify the number of tickets you wish to purchase. Spaces may be purchased as a group or by each individual. If you would like the room to yourself, you may purchase additional slots, otherwise additional guests may join your game. Each room presents an entirely new set of challenges and mysteries. For specific room details, visit the Room page.
